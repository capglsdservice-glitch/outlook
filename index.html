About The Salon

A grooming space built with men in mind

Since 2022, Outlook Men's Hair Dresser has been Thevaram's go-to address for a proper haircut, a sharp beard line and a bit of quiet in the middle of a busy day. Every chair, every mirror, every product on our shelves is chosen for one purpose — to help you leave looking like the best version of yourself.

  • Established in 2022, trusted across Thevaram & Theni
  • Trained, experienced barbers and stylists
  • Hygienic tools, fresh towels, sanitised stations
  • Premium, salon-grade products only
  • A calm, relaxing atmosphere — even a kids' chair for the little ones
